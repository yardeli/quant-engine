Research Integration Notes

This engine incorporates findings from the deep research conducted on quantitative trading strategies.

Core Strategies (01)

  • Trend Following — Implemented via TS Momentum (Moskowitz et al.) with fast/slow signal blend
  • Mean Reversion — OU process calibration with half-life estimation & z-score entry/exit
  • Cross-Sectional Momentum — Jegadeesh-Titman 12-1 month momentum with skip-month
  • Regime-Aware — Vol break model scales exposure during volatility spikes (ADX-equivalent)
  • Statistical Arbitrage — Engle-Granger cointegration pairs with dynamic hedge ratios

Machine Learning (02)

  • Gradient Boosting — Walk-forward GBM with cross-sectional z-score features
  • Feature Engineering — 32+ features: momentum, volatility, mean reversion, volume, cross-asset
  • Overfitting Prevention — Winsorized targets, regularization, walk-forward retraining
  • Ensemble Approach — Multiple models combined via inverse-vol or performance weighting

Risk Management (03)

  • Volatility Targeting — Portfolio scaled to target annualized vol (most important technique)
  • VaR/CVaR Limits — Historical simulation VaR with automatic position scaling
  • Drawdown Protection — Progressive de-risking: linear scale-down starting at 50% of max DD
  • Position Limits — Max 15% single name, max 1.5x gross leverage, ±30% net exposure
  • Concentration — Herfindahl index monitoring for portfolio diversification

Technical Indicators (04)

  • RSI — Wilder's RSI as feature for ML & mean reversion signals
  • Bollinger Bands — Band position as feature for regime detection
  • Parkinson Volatility — High-low range estimator (more efficient than close-to-close)
  • Volume Features — Relative volume & VWAP trend for confirmation signals
  • Beta & Correlation — Rolling SPY beta for cross-asset risk decomposition

Backtesting (05)

  • Walk-Forward — No future data leakage; features computed with past data only
  • Realistic Costs — Commission + spread + Almgren-Chriss market impact model
  • Risk-Adjusted Metrics — Sharpe, Sortino, Calmar, profit factor, skewness/kurtosis
  • Health Scorecard — Research-defined targets: WR≥50%, PF≥1.5, Sharpe≥1.0, DD≤20%
